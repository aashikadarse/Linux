move command (mv)- to move files and directories from one location to another
  mv <source> <destination>
  eg:mv app.css styles/

Renaming :we can use mv commnad to rename files and Folders
          mv <current> <new name>
          mv chickens.txt roosters.txt

move and rename: move and rename at same time 
                 mv <filename> <destination>/ <newnamw>
              eg:mv notes.txt docs/notes1.txt
